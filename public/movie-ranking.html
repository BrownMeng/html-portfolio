The Best Movies According to Matt

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

fantastic trilogy of films, that i would recommend watching in succession as it is a continuopus retelling of the narrative of the books published by J.R.R Tolkien
However, be warned the extended editions are very long and can take a whole day to watch. so either plan ahead or prepare alot of snacks and get cosy

Big Hero 6

An animated film adaptation of a long standing comic series, in which deviates from the standard productions made by disney, personally one of my favourites due to Baymax

Pursuit of Happiness

A heartwrenching story telling the events of a man trying to make his way in life and support his family, with the portrayal of the main character being played by Will Smith
